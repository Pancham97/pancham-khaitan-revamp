_Guest writer: [Komal Rangoonwala](https://komalrangoonwala.com)_

Unlock yourself through the power of your subconscious mind. Let it know that you are grateful for what you have. Be grateful for the fact that you are alive. Be grateful for you can read this. Be grateful for everything that you have and are going to have, for what you are and are going to become.
